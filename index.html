Biography

I am currently a Ph.D student at University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Jiansong Deng, and Prof. Jingrun Chen. Before that, I received my Master's degree of Computing Mathematics from Hefei University of Technology in 2019, and my Bachelor’s degree of Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Fuyang Normal University in 2016.
